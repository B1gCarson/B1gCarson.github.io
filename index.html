About Me

Hi there! My name is Carson Spencer, and I'm currently a senior at the University of South Carolina studying Sport and Entertainment Management. Being a college student has been a great experience for me, and I've enjoyed being part of such a vibrant and diverse community. I'm 21 years old, and I was born and raised in Columbia, SC, so I've always had a strong connection to this area. In my free time, I have a variety of hobbies that keep me busy. I enjoy lifting weights and playing pickup basketball to stay active, but I also love playing video games and having deep conversations about music. I'm a big movie fan too, so you'll often find me catching up on the latest releases.

One of the things that's most important to me is giving back to my community, and that's why I'm a Young Life leader at Dreher High School. Young Life is a Christian ministry that reaches out to students in middle school, high school, and college, and it's been an incredibly rewarding experience for me to be involved with this organization. I'm passionate about helping young people grow and develop, and it's been a joy to be part of their lives and support them through their challenges. I believe that we all have a responsibility to use our gifts and talents to make the world a better place, and being a Young Life leader is just one way that I try to live out that value.
